Our Story

A neighborhood spot,
built on community.

Atlantic Bagel & Coffee Co. has been a cornerstone of the South Shore since the 1990s, with beloved locations in Hingham and Cohasset. More than just a bagel shop, it's where neighbors greet neighbors, where regulars are remembered by name, and where locals come for a warm, familiar start to the day.

The bagels are baked fresh every single morning — you can order rosemary olive oil, asiago, pumpernickel, everything, cinnamon sugar, and more. Cream cheese comes in flavors you won't find at any chain: jalapeño, olive, scallion, walnut raisin, sun-dried tomato, and herb. Get your coffee and your cup back later for a free refill.

It's cash only. It's a little no-frills. And it's absolutely worth it — the line out the door on a Saturday morning will tell you everything.
